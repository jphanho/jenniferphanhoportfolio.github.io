Nijigasaki! Crash!! Course!!!

Love Live! Nijigasaki School Idol Club is a spin-off of the Japanese multi-media series Love Live! It follows the story of 12 girls as they pursue their dreams as individual solo idols. The story of these characters are explored in the anime sharing the same title as well as the game, Love Live! School Idol Festival All Stars (SIFAS). I will introduce six of my favorite characters from the series, Kasumi, Ai, Karin, Lanzhu, Shioriko, and Mia.
