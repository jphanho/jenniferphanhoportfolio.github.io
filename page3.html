Who is Karin Asaka?

Karin is a third year student at Nijigasaki high school. While she was initially hesitant to join the school idol club due to her competitive and ambitious spirit causing conflict working with others in the past, their emphasis on individuality and inspiring each other to reach greater heights won her over. Karin also works as an amateur model and uses her experience as a model and an idol to enhance her performance in both areas.
